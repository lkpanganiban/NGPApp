National Greening Program

A project which gears to make the Philippines a greener country.

Our Mission And Vision
  • The National Greening Program (NGP) seeks the attainment of sustainable human development, and economic and ecological security.   As a strategy, it aspires the planting of some 1.5 billion trees in around 1.5 million hectares of public lands for six years, starting 2011 to 2016.
